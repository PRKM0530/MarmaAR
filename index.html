About the Project

Integrating Classical Texts with Modern Tracking

The Marma system, as documented in the Sushruta Samhita, describes 107 critical anatomical intersections across the human body. This application brings that ancient topographical map into the modern era. By leveraging localized MediaPipe tracking, MarmaAR dynamically scales each point to the individual user using the traditional Angula biometric unit, ensuring high spatial accuracy for educational reference.

  • Real-time 33-landmark pose estimation (MediaPipe)
  • Dynamically scaled to your unique body proportions
  • 4-tier injury severity classification
  • Offline-capable PWA
  • Auto front / back view detection
